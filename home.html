About

We are a student-led Cybersecurity Club focused on building skills, sharing knowledge, and growing a community around digital security. From hands-on CTF (Capture the Flag) competitions to practical workshops and expert-led talks, we offer opportunities for all skill levels to learn about ethical hacking, online safety, and real-world security challenges. Our goal is to make cybersecurity exciting and accessible—whether you're just getting started or looking to sharpen your skills. Join us to explore the world of hacking (the good kind), work on cool projects, and meet others who are just as passionate as you are.
